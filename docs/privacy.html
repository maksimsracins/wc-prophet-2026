PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 8, 2026

WC Prophet 2026 ("the App") is an unofficial fan prediction app for the 2026 football tournament, developed independently by Maksims Racins. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by FIFA or any football organization. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your picks and bracket data are stored locally on your device only.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (country/region)
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage information

All ads are requested with non-personalized ads settings enabled. For more information on how Google uses data, visit: policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. All game data (your picks) exists only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App or use the "Start Over" feature.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

The App integrates Google AdMob. Please review Google's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
